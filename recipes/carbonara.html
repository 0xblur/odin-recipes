Classic Carbonara

Description

This is the classic carbonara recipe.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti
  2. Add olive oil
  3. Add bacon
  4. Add onion
  5. Add garlic
  6. Add salt
  7. Add pepper
  8. Add eggs
  9. Add parsley
  10. Cook
